We collect personal information from you, including information about your: name, contact information, interactions with us and allergies and relevant health concerns.

We collect your personal information in order to: confirm our guests' reservations, and contact them if a booking ever needs to be amended or discussed, be made aware of any allergies before the booking date- to ensure there is no cross contamination in the kitchen.

Providing some information is optional. However, if you choose not to enter your name and phone number, we'll be unable to confirm the booking, due to excessive cancellations.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and only allowing the owners of the restaurant to access it to confirm bookings.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at contact@haukai_restaurant.co.nz, or 09 401 4019, or write to us at 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands.
